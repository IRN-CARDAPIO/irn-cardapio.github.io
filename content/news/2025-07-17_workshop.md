### DREAM hybrid workshop: July 16-17th, 2025 in Toulouse, France and  online .

We were pleased to hold a DREAM course & workshop on July 16th and 17th 2025 in LEGOS/OMP, Toulouse and on-line!
We have introduced the DREAM model to about 17 participants (6 in person in Toulouse and  11 on-line) from Brazil, Vietnam, Benin, CÃ´te d'Ivoire, Bangladesh and France. Time lag from Brazil to Vietnam (11-hour lag) made it a challenge !
